What is the difference?

Students will be able to compare US families and Chinese families.
After learning about families, do you think US families and Chinese families are the same or different? Or maybe they have both similarities and differences, and it is hard to cover everything in just one sentence. Then, let’s talk about it! Read this material to supplement your knowledge of Chinese families. Below are some questions to think about while you read. After you read, let’s compare families in the US and in China. You can show us your conclusion in a diagram or a chart.

  • How do they address their siblings? Directly by their names?
  • Do grandparents live with them?
  • What about pets? Do a lot of families have pets? Do they consider pets as family members?
  • How about the family size? Does it stay the same over the years or not? If not, what’s the trend?
